Conway's Game of Life

How to Use:

  • Make a Cell Live: Tap on a cell to make it live.
  • Kill a Cell: Tap on a live cell to turn it off.
  • Change Speed: Use the controllers on the top right to adjust the simulation speed.
  • Use a Pre-built Template: Click the button on the top left to select a pre-built template.
  • Zoom In/Out: Use the mouse wheel to zoom in or out of the grid.
  • Pan the View: Right-click and drag to move around the grid.
  • Change Rules: Click the "See Rules" button to modify the rules for cell survival.
  • Play-Pause-Reset:Use the controls at the bottom center of the screen to play pause or reset the game
